About

Music has always been an integral part of my life. As a kid, I would scribble down songs, mostly keeping them to myself. During my teenage years, I joined a few bands and started creating original music. Back then, making music felt like solving a puzzle by matching words with melodies and rhythms.

Immigrating to the US was a turning point. I started busking and sharing my songs. Music slowly became integral to me, helping me navigate tough times, connect with people, and make sense of life's ups and downs.

The songs, albums, and EPs I create are my ways of sharing my experiences as authentically as possible.
